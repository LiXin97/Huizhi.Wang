Biography

Huizhi Wang received the B.Eng. degree in Telecommunication Engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU), Xi'an, China, in 2021, and the M.Eng. degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Southeast University (SEU), Nanjing, China, in 2024. During her master's studies, she was a Research Assistant at the Huawei-SEU Joint Laboratory, working on integrated sensing and communication systems for future 6G networks.

She is currently pursuing her Ph.D. degree in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Chau Yuen and Prof. Emil Björnson (KTH Royal Institute of Technology). Her research focuses on extremely large-scale MIMO systems, near-field communications, and integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) technologies. She has published several papers at top international conferences and journals, including IEEE ICC and IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing. She received multiple National Scholarships and was recognized as an Outstanding Graduate of Shaanxi Province.
